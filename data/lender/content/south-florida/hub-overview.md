## What Makes South Florida Lending Different

South Florida isn't one market — it's a stack of micro-markets glued together by I-95 and ambition.

**Coastal & insurance reality.** Wind mitigation credits, flood zones, and HO-6 wall-in policies aren't fine print here — they're the difference between approval and a last-minute denial. Lenders who close in Weston, Boca, or Miami Beach weekly know which insurance assumptions to build into your pre-approval before you tour homes.

**International & diverse buyers.** ITIN loans, foreign national programs, and cross-border asset verification aren't niche products in Miami-Dade and Broward — they're Tuesday. The brokers profiled here built their practices around that reality.

**Condo complexity.** High-rise approval lists, recertification timelines, and HOA litigation reviews slow down — or kill — deals every week. You want a loan officer who's navigated a Miami-Dade limited-review denial and found a path forward.

**Speed wins offers.** In competitive pockets from Coral Gables to Delray, a clean pre-approval and a lender who hits FAR/BAR financing deadlines often matters more than shaving an eighth off the rate.

**Military & veterans.** From Homestead to Pembroke Pines to MacDill commuters, VA expertise is a genuine specialty — not a checkbox on a product menu.

**Luxury & jumbo.** Palm Beach and the coastal Broward corridor regularly blow past conforming limits. Wholesale access and portfolio products matter.
